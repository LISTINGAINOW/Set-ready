# SetVenue Launches Production Marketplace

**Los Angeles, CA — [Launch Date Placeholder]** — SetVenue today announced the launch of its production marketplace, a platform built to help production teams discover and book locations, crew housing, and event venues in one place.

SetVenue is designed for productions that face friction from juggling multiple platforms. The marketplace gives hosts a simple way to list properties with 0% fees and gives producers a cleaner path from scouting to booking.

---

## Key Features

- **Unified platform** — Locations, stays, and events in one place
- **Lower fees** — 10% guest fee vs 15-25% on competing platforms
- **Production-first design** — Built for film, photo, and event workflows
- **Transparent pricing** — Clear fees upfront, no hidden markups
- **0% host fees** — Property owners keep more of their earnings

---

## Market Position

Production teams currently navigate multiple platforms for a single project:
- Giggster for locations (15-25% fees)
- Airbnb for housing (hosts often reject productions)
- Peerspace for events (~20% fees)

SetVenue consolidates all three use cases with lower fees and production-specific features.
