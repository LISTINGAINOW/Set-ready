## Why Partner with SetVenue

SetVenue offers a unified platform for locations, crew housing, and event venues — addressing a gap that forces production teams to juggle multiple platforms for a single project.

### Why teams choose SetVenue

- **One platform** — Locations, stays, and events in one place
- **Lower fees** — 10% guest fee vs Giggster's 15-25%
- **Production-first** — Built for production workflows, not generic rentals
- **Transparent pricing** — Clear fees upfront, no hidden markups
