## Purpose

This Confidentiality Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into to protect confidential information shared in connection with SetVenue privacy-tier listings or NDA-required properties.

---

## 1. Definition of Confidential Information

"Confidential Information" includes but is not limited to:

- Property address and location details
- Owner/Host contact information
- Property photos not publicly listed
- Pricing and booking terms
- Any information marked as confidential

---

## 2. Obligations of Receiving Party

Receiving Party agrees to:

- Keep all Confidential Information strictly confidential
- Not disclose any Confidential Information to third parties
- Use Confidential Information solely for evaluating or booking the Property
- Not photograph, record, or document confidential aspects of the Property without permission
- Return or destroy all Confidential Information upon request

---

## 3. Exceptions

This Agreement does not apply to information that:

- Is or becomes publicly available through no fault of Receiving Party
- Was already known to Receiving Party prior to disclosure
- Is independently developed without use of Confidential Information
- Is required to be disclosed by law or court order

---

## 4. Duration

This Agreement shall remain in effect for **two (2) years** from the date of signing, or until the Confidential Information becomes publicly available, whichever occurs first.

---

## 5. Remedies

Receiving Party acknowledges that any breach of this Agreement may cause irreparable harm to Disclosing Party. Disclosing Party shall be entitled to injunctive relief and any other remedies available at law or equity.
