# SetVenue Customer Response Guide

**Purpose:** Handle replies to outreach emails and inbound inquiries.
**Rule:** Respond within 2 hours during business hours. Same day always.

---

## Positive Replies

### "I'm interested, tell me more"
→ Keep it short. Don't oversell.

"Thanks for getting back to me! Here's the quick version:

1. You list your property at setvenue.com/free-listing (takes ~10 minutes)
2. Production companies browse and request to book
3. You approve or decline every request
4. They pay through our platform — you get 100% of your listed price

I can also set up the listing for you if you send me a few photos and your property details. Whatever's easier.

Any questions?"

### "How do I sign up?"
→ Direct them straight to the listing page.

"Head to setvenue.com/free-listing — takes about 10 minutes. Upload your best photos, set your price, and you're live.

If you get stuck on anything, just reply here and I'll help."

### "Can we do a quick call?"
→ Always say yes. Keep it under 15 minutes.

"Absolutely — what day/time works best for you? I'm flexible this week."

After the call, follow up with:
"Great talking with you! Here's the link to get listed: setvenue.com/free-listing. Let me know if you need anything."

### "I manage X properties, can we do a bulk deal?"
→ THIS IS A TIER 1 LEAD. Priority response.

"That's exactly the kind of partnership we're looking for. I'd love to get your properties featured on the platform.

For bulk listings, I can set everything up for you — just send me a spreadsheet or list with property addresses, photos (or Google Drive links), and pricing preferences.

Want to jump on a quick 15-minute call this week to map it out?"

### "I'm already on Giggster/Peerspace"
→ Position as complementary, not competitive.

"Smart — you already know the market works. SetVenue is just an additional channel for you.

The main difference: we don't charge you anything. Giggster takes 15-25% from owners. We take 0%. So you're making more per booking with us.

Most hosts list on multiple platforms. No exclusivity required."

## Neutral Replies

### "Maybe later" / "Not right now"
→ Respect it. Plant the seed.

"Totally understand — no rush at all. The free listing offer is good for our first 500 properties, so whenever you're ready, just head to setvenue.com/free-listing.

I'll follow up in a month to see if anything's changed. Have a great week!"

### "Send me more info"
→ Don't send a PDF or attachment. Keep it in the email.

"Here's the quick overview:

**What:** SetVenue is a marketplace for film productions, photo shoots, and events to book unique properties.

**For you:** List your property for free. Set your own price. Keep 100% of it. We charge the renter a 10% fee.

**How:** Sign up at setvenue.com/free-listing (10 minutes).

**Why now:** We're giving free listings to our first 500 property owners (6 months, no commitment).

Happy to answer any specific questions!"

## Negative Replies

### "Not interested"
→ One line. Don't argue.

"No worries — thanks for letting me know. If anything changes, setvenue.com is always there. Have a great day!"

### "Stop emailing me" / "Unsubscribe"
→ Immediate removal. No questions.

"Done — removed from our list. Apologies for the inconvenience."

Then: Remove from all send lists immediately. Add to do-not-contact list.

### "This is spam"
→ Same as unsubscribe, but also check our practices.

"Sincere apologies — you've been removed. This won't happen again."

Then: Remove immediately. Review how they got on the list. If our scraping added them incorrectly, fix the process.

## Inbound Inquiries (from the website)

### Production company wants to book
→ Priority. Respond within 1 hour.

"Thanks for reaching out! I'd be happy to help you find the right space.

Could you share a few details?
- What type of production? (film, photo, event, etc.)
- What date(s) are you looking at?
- How many people in your crew?
- Any must-have features? (pool, kitchen, outdoor, etc.)
- Budget range?

I'll match you with our best options."

### Property owner wants to list
→ High priority. They came to us.

"Welcome! Glad you found us.

You can get listed in about 10 minutes at setvenue.com/free-listing. Upload your best photos, set your price, and you'll be live for production companies to find.

As one of our first 500 property owners, your listing is completely free for 6 months.

Need help? Just reply here — I can walk you through it or set it up for you."

## Tone Rules

- **Be human.** Not corporate.
- **Be brief.** Respect their time.
- **Be helpful.** Answer the actual question.
- **Never be defensive.** Even if they're rude.
- **Never oversell.** State facts, let them decide.
- **Always end with a clear next step.** Link, question, or call.
