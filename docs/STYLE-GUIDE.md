# SetVenue Style Guide

**Purpose:** Keep all content — site, emails, social, docs — sounding like the same person.

---

## Brand Voice

**We sound like:** A smart friend who works in production and found a better way.
**We don't sound like:** A corporation. A sales pitch. A tech bro.

### Traits
- **Direct** — say it in fewer words
- **Honest** — if something isn't built yet, say so
- **Confident** — we know our product is good, no need to oversell
- **Casual** — contractions are fine. Fragments too.
- **Warm** — we actually care about property owners AND production companies

### Examples

❌ "SetVenue is a revolutionary platform designed to disrupt the production location marketplace by leveraging cutting-edge technology."

✅ "SetVenue connects production companies with incredible properties. Owners keep 100%. Simple."

❌ "We are delighted to announce the launch of our premium listing service."

✅ "We just launched. Here's how it works."

❌ "Unlock the full potential of your property's monetization capabilities."

✅ "Your property could be earning from film shoots."

## Writing Rules

### General
- Active voice over passive ("We charge renters" not "Renters are charged")
- Short paragraphs (2-3 sentences max)
- One idea per paragraph
- No jargon unless talking to industry people
- No exclamation marks in subject lines or headlines
- Use numbers, not words ("10% fee" not "ten percent fee")

### Emails
- Under 150 words for cold outreach
- Under 50 words for DMs
- Sign off as "Josh" or "Josh Feuer" — not "The SetVenue Team"
- No attachments. No PDFs. Just text and links.
- One clear CTA per email (usually "setvenue.com/free-listing")

### Website
- Headlines: under 8 words
- Subheads: explain the headline in one sentence
- Body: short paragraphs, bullet points for features
- CTAs: action-oriented ("List Your Property" not "Learn More")

### Social Media
- Instagram: casual, visual-first, 2-3 relevant hashtags per post (not 30)
- Twitter/X: one-liner hooks, threads for education
- LinkedIn: more professional, industry-focused
- No emoji spam. One emoji per post max in captions. None in headlines.

## Terminology

### Always Use
- **Property owner** (not "host" unless talking to Airbnb people)
- **Production company** or **renter** (not "guest" or "client")
- **Booking** (not "reservation")
- **Service fee** (not "commission" or "platform fee")
- **List** or **listing** (not "register" or "sign up" for properties)

### Never Use
- "Passive income" — sounds like a scam
- "Monetize" — too corporate
- "Disrupt" — overused
- "Exciting opportunity" — instant delete
- "Dear Sir/Madam" — 1995 called
- "To whom it may concern" — same energy
- "We're excited to" — nobody cares about your excitement
- "Don't miss out" — too salesy
- "Act now" — infomercial energy
- "Unlock" or "leverage" or "synergy" — banned forever

## Numbers

- **Our fee:** 10% service fee (charged to renter)
- **Owner fee:** 0% (they keep everything)
- **Competitor comparison:** Giggster 15-25%, Peerspace 15-20%
- **Free offer:** First 500 properties, 6 months
- **Properties live:** 4 (update as this grows)
- **Cities:** 6 (LA, Atlanta, NYC, Austin, Miami, Nashville)

## Colors & Visual Identity

- **Primary:** Dark theme (matches site)
- **Accent:** Whatever the site uses
- **Photography:** Real property photos. No stock images. Ever.
- **Graphics:** Clean typography on dark backgrounds for value prop posts

## Logo & Name

- **Full name:** SetVenue
- **Capitalization:** SetVenue (capital S, capital V, one word)
- **Never:** Set Venue (two words), SETVENUE (all caps), setvenue (all lowercase — except in URLs)
- **Domain:** setvenue.com
- **Email from:** noreply@setvenue.com or hello@setvenue.com
