## Why This Works (In Terms Mom Understands)

### 1. You Already Do This Job
You've been matching properties to productions for 20 years. SetVenue just puts it online where MORE people can find you. Instead of getting inquiries one by one through your network, now anyone in the country can search for a "Malibu mansion with a pool for a photo shoot" and find YOUR properties.

### 2. It Costs Property Owners Nothing
- **Zero fees for owners.** They keep 100% of their price.
- Giggster charges owners 15%. Peerspace charges 15-25%.
- We charge the renter 10%. That's it.
- **This makes owners WANT to list with us** instead of the competition.

### 3. Your 1,003 Properties Are the Unfair Advantage
- Giggster took years to build their inventory
- We have 1,003 properties from Day 1 — because of YOU
- That's not something money can buy. That's 20 years of relationships.

### 4. The Money Is Real
- Average film location booking: $1,500-$5,000/day
- Our cut: 10% = $150-$500 per booking
- 50 bookings a month = $7,500-$25,000/month
- 100 bookings = $15,000-$50,000/month
- **And that's before crew housing and other services**
