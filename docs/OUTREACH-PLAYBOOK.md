# SetVenue Outreach Playbook

**Last updated:** March 25, 2026
**Goal:** Fill 500 free listings in 6 months
**Status:** 4 / 500 filled

---

## The Offer

- Free listing for 6 months (first 500 only)
- No credit card, no commitment
- Owner keeps 100% of their rental price
- We charge the renter a 10% booking fee
- Competitors (Giggster) charge owners 15-25%

## Voice & Tone

**Sound like:** A founder who personally found their property and thinks it's a fit. Casual, direct, human.
**Don't sound like:** A mass email. A sales robot. A corporation.
**Always use:** "I" not "we" in cold outreach. Josh is the founder talking, not a company.
**Keep it short:** Under 150 words for cold emails. Under 50 for DMs. People scan, they don't read.
**No attachments.** No PDFs. No pitch decks. Just text and a link.

## Key Selling Points

Use these in every conversation — memorize them:

1. **"You keep 100%"** — we charge the renter, not you
2. **"Production bookings pay 2-5x vacation rental rates"** — bigger money per booking
3. **"Daytime only, less wear and tear"** — most shoots don't need overnight
4. **"You're already set up"** — for Airbnb/VRBO hosts, zero extra work
5. **"First 500 free"** — urgency, scarcity, FOMO
6. **"10 minutes to list"** — low friction
7. **"Giggster charges 15-25%, we charge 0%"** — direct competitor comparison

**Never say:**
- "Passive income" (sounds like a scam)
- "Monetize your property" (too corporate)
- "Unlock value" (meaningless)
- "Exciting opportunity" (instant delete)

## Who We're Talking To

Understand the person before you pitch:

### Property Management Companies
- **They care about:** Revenue per property, client retention, low effort
- **They worry about:** Liability, damage, managing another platform
- **Speak their language:** "Revenue on idle days," "no operational overhead," "we handle everything"

### Airbnb/VRBO Superhosts
- **They care about:** Occupancy rate, revenue per night, reviews
- **They worry about:** Cannibalization of existing bookings, platform conflicts
- **Speak their language:** "Additional channel," "production crews pay premium," "different audience entirely"

### Real Estate Agents
- **They care about:** Commission, client relationships, looking smart
- **They worry about:** Recommending something that wastes their client's time
- **Speak their language:** "Your clients earn more," "you look like a hero," "easy referral"

### Direct Owners
- **They care about:** Easy money, protecting their home, control
- **They worry about:** Strangers, damage, complexity
- **Speak their language:** "You approve every booking," "crews carry insurance," "10 minutes to set up"

## Target Priority (in order)

### 🥇 Tier 1: Property Management Companies
- **Why:** One deal = 20-100 properties
- **Template:** `cold-outreach-management`
- **Where to find:** Google "property management [city]", LinkedIn, Yelp, Buildium directories
- **Cities:** LA, Atlanta, NYC, Austin, Miami, Nashville
- **Pitch angle:** "Extra revenue from days properties sit empty"
- **Goal:** 10 management companies = 200+ properties

### 🥈 Tier 2: Airbnb/VRBO Superhosts
- **Why:** Already set up for short-term rental, easy convert
- **Template:** `cold-outreach-host`
- **Where to find:** Airbnb search (luxury, 10+ photos, superhost badge), VRBO
- **Pitch angle:** "Additional revenue channel, zero extra work"
- **Goal:** 50 superhosts = 50-150 properties

### 🥉 Tier 3: Real Estate Agents
- **Why:** Referral multiplier — they know dozens of owners
- **Template:** `cold-outreach-agent`
- **Where to find:** Zillow agent profiles, Realtor.com, LinkedIn
- **Pitch angle:** "Help your clients earn more from their properties"
- **Goal:** 20 agents referring 3-5 clients each = 60-100 properties

### Tier 4: Direct Property Owners
- **Why:** Highest quality but slowest
- **Template:** `cold-outreach-owner`
- **Where to find:** Zillow/Redfin (luxury homes), Google Maps, driving neighborhoods
- **Pitch angle:** "Your property could earn from shoots you'd never know about"
- **Goal:** Fill remaining spots

## Email Sequence

| Day | Template | Purpose |
|-----|----------|---------|
| 0 | Cold outreach (owner/agent/management/host) | First touch |
| 3 | follow-up-1 | Bump — "did you see my note?" |
| 7 | follow-up-2 | Last chance — urgency |

**Rules:**
- Max 50 emails/day (Resend free tier)
- Never email the same person twice in 48 hours
- Stop sequence if they reply (positive OR negative)
- Track everything in send-log.json
- **Best send times:** Tuesday-Thursday, 9-11 AM recipient's timezone
- **Worst send times:** Monday morning (buried), Friday afternoon (checked out), weekends

## Subject Line A/B Tests

Each template has a `subject` and `subject_b`. Alternate between them:
- Odd-numbered sends → subject A
- Even-numbered sends → subject B
- Track open rates if possible, double down on winner

**Subject line rules:**
- Under 6 words is ideal
- Use their city or property address for personalization
- No ALL CAPS, no exclamation marks, no emojis in subject
- Question format outperforms statement format

## Objection Handling

**"I already use Giggster/Peerspace"**
→ "Great — you already know the market. We're an additional channel with lower fees. List on both and see which performs better. Most hosts do."

**"I don't want strangers in my house"**
→ "Totally understand. Production crews are professional — they carry insurance, sign agreements, and treat properties carefully. Plus you approve every booking. If it's not a fit, you just decline."

**"Sounds too good to be free"**
→ "Fair question. We're building inventory so production companies have enough options to book through us. The free period lets you try it risk-free. After 500 properties, standard pricing applies."

**"I don't have time to manage another platform"**
→ "Listing takes 10 minutes. After that, bookings come to you via email — accept or decline. That's it. No calendar syncing, no messaging back and forth."

**"What about insurance/liability?"**
→ "Renters are required to carry production insurance. We verify this before confirming bookings. We also have a dispute resolution process and cancellation policies that protect you."

**"What kind of productions?"**
→ "Film crews, photographers, brand campaigns, music videos, corporate events, podcasts. It depends on your space — a modern kitchen attracts food shoots, a big backyard attracts events. You'll be surprised what people book."

**"How do I know I'll actually get bookings?"**
→ "I can't guarantee bookings — that depends on your property and pricing. But we're actively bringing production companies to the platform and your listing is free, so there's zero downside to trying."

**"Can I see other listings first?"**
→ "Absolutely — browse setvenue.com to see what's live. You'll get a feel for how properties are presented."

## What Success Looks Like

**Week 1:** 50 emails sent, 5 replies, 1-2 listings
**Week 2:** 100 total emails, 10 replies, 5 listings
**Month 1:** 500 emails, 50 replies, 20-30 listings
**Month 2:** 1,000 emails, 100 replies, 75-100 listings
**Month 3:** Referrals start compounding, 150-200 listings

These are conservative estimates. One management company deal could add 30-50 in a single week.

## Escalation Paths

**If someone wants to talk on the phone:**
→ Josh takes the call. Keep it under 15 minutes. Have the playbook selling points memorized.

**If a management company is interested:**
→ Priority lead. Offer to personally onboard their first 5 properties for them. White glove treatment.

**If someone wants a contract/agreement:**
→ Send them to setvenue.com/legal/owner-agreement. It's already live.

**If someone reports an issue or complaint:**
→ Respond within 2 hours. Be human. Fix it. Document in send-log.

## Tracking

- **Leads:** `~/.openclaw/workspace/research/leads-YYYY-MM-DD.json`
- **Send log:** `setready/data/send-log.json`
- **Weekly outreach list:** `~/.openclaw/workspace/research/weekly-outreach-list.json`
- **Metrics:** Track sends, replies, conversions, unsubscribes

### Key Metrics to Watch
- **Reply rate:** Target 10%+ (healthy cold email is 5-15%)
- **Conversion rate:** Target 20% of replies → listing (1-2% of total sends)
- **Unsubscribe/complaint rate:** Must stay under 0.5% or Resend will flag us
- **Best performing template:** Double down on what works
- **Best performing city:** Focus outreach there

## Daily Outreach Rhythm

**Morning (15 min):**
1. Check `weekly-outreach-list.json` for ranked leads
2. Pick top 20-30 unsent leads
3. Match to correct template (management/host/agent/owner)
4. Personalize: add property address, city, company name
5. Send via Resend API (or email campaign skill)
6. Log in send-log.json

**Afternoon (5 min):**
7. Check for replies
8. Respond to interested leads immediately
9. Update send-log with reply status

**Weekly (Monday):**
10. Review metrics: sends, replies, conversions
11. Adjust templates based on what's working
12. Follow up on day 3 and day 7 leads
