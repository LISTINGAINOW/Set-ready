## Key Differentiators

### vs Giggster (our main competitor)
- **We charge owners 0%. They charge 15-25%.** This is the killer.
- They have more inventory — but owners are leaving because of high fees
- We offer events + extended stays. They're photo/film only.
- We have compliance tools (TOT, insurance tracking). They don't.
- **Use this line:** "You keep every dollar on SetVenue. On Giggster, you lose 15-25% of every booking."

### vs Peerspace
- **We charge owners 0%. They charge 15-20%.**
- They're strong in events. We cover events + film + extended stays.
- Similar positioning but we undercut on fees.
- **Use this line:** "Same idea, but you keep 100% of your price with us."

### vs Airbnb
- We're built for productions, not vacations. Different audience entirely.
- Production bookings pay 2-5x vacation rates.
- We require production insurance. Airbnb doesn't.
- We're complementary — list on both, different customer base.
- **Use this line:** "Think of us as Airbnb for film crews. Different audience, higher rates, and your Airbnb listing stays active."

## When They Object

**"Giggster has more properties"**
→ "They do — and they charge owners 15-25% for it. We're growing fast because owners keep 100%. More properties join every week."

**"Nobody's heard of you"**
→ "We're new — launched this month. That's exactly why we're offering free listings to our first 500 owners. Early movers get the most visibility."

**"What if you shut down?"**
→ "Your listing is free. Your photos and content are yours. If we disappeared tomorrow (we won't), you've lost nothing. But right now, you're missing out on production bookings you'd never get otherwise."

**"I'll wait until you're bigger"**
→ "Owners who list now get the most visibility — less competition, featured placement, and a free listing. Once we hit 500, standard pricing kicks in."
