FAQ

Common questions, answered clearly.

Discreet Set is built for privacy-sensitive productions, so the booking questions tend to be more specific than a generic marketplace. That’s the point.

Question 1

How does booking work on Discreet Set?

Browse locations, review pricing and privacy tier, request or select available time, and complete checkout once the booking is approved. Exact address details may be limited until the privacy requirements for that listing are met.

Question 2

What do the privacy tiers mean?

Public listings share more upfront detail. Discrete listings limit identifying information until a serious inquiry is made. NDA Required listings may require a signed confidentiality agreement before exact address details, additional photos, or sensitive production notes are released.

Question 3

What payment methods do you support?

Payment support depends on the processor connected to the platform. The booking flow is designed for professional production payments, transparent fees, and adult-friendly compliance rather than consumer-grade shortcuts that create risk for the business.

Question 4

What is the cancellation policy?

Unless a listing says otherwise, cancellations should be made as early as possible. Refunds, credits, or rescheduling depend on the booking terms shown at checkout, the host policy, and whether the cancellation affects a confirmed production date.

Question 5

How does the NDA process work?

For NDA Required listings, producers may be asked to sign a confidentiality agreement before receiving exact property details. This helps protect hosts who need stricter privacy around address disclosure, interiors, or repeat-use production spaces.

Question 6

What are the requirements to host on Discreet Set?

Hosts should control the property or have permission to list it, provide accurate photos and rules, disclose access limitations, set realistic privacy expectations, and be comfortable receiving adult-friendly production inquiries that still must remain legal and professional.
