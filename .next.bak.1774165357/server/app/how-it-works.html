How it works

Straightforward booking for a category that usually isn’t.

Discreet Set is meant to compress the scouting process without flattening the privacy needs that make this market different.

Step 1

Browse locations

Search by city, property type, price, and privacy tier to find spaces that fit your shoot and your level of discretion.

Step 2

Check availability

Review listing details, booking minimums, and available dates. For tighter privacy tiers, some details unlock later in the inquiry process.

Step 3

Book & pay

Submit your request, confirm the production details, and complete payment through the booking workflow once the reservation is approved.

Step 4

Create

Show up with the right expectations, follow the house rules, and use a location that was actually built for your production needs.

What stays consistent

Transparent hourly pricing and booking minimums

Privacy tiers that explain what gets shared and when

A cleaner path from scouting to confirmed production day
